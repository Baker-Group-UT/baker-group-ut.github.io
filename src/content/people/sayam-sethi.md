Sayam is a PhD candidate in the ECE Department at The University of Texas at Austin. He is interested in architectural design for realising Fault-Tolerant Quantum Computers (FTQC), with a specific focus on scheduling realtime operations, and minimizing program runtime. Before starting his PhD, he obtained his B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Delhi, where he was working with Dr. Preeti Ranjan Panda on 3D DRAM.
